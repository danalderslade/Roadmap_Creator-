Quick Instructions

  1. Download the Excel template and fill one roadmap item per row.
  2. Use the core fields: application, feature_name, region, month, year, delivery_type.
  3. Quarter is auto-generated from month and year.
  4. Optional `system` column enables richer swimlanes.
  5. Upload the file as CSV or Excel (.xlsx) and click Generate Roadmap.
  6. Use filters to slice by application, feature, system, region, delivery type, quarter, month, and year.
